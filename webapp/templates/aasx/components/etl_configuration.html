What is Federated Learning?

Federated learning allows your data to contribute to improving AI models without sharing raw data. Your data stays on your system while contributing to collaborative learning.

  • Privacy Preserved: Raw data never leaves your system
  • Better Models: Contribute to improved AI performance
  • Collaborative: Learn from diverse data sources
  • Opt-out Anytime: Change consent settings at any time
